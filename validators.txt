Available osmlint processors:

osmlint bridgeonnode

  Identifies invalid nodes with bridge=* tags and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint bridgeonnode --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint filterdate

  Filters features added/modified and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint filterdate --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint filterusers

  Filters features touched by a set of users and sends them to stdout. Configure the list of users in [users.json](/validators/filterUsers/users.json)

  osmlint filterusers --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint missinghighwaysus

  Compares OpenStreetMap to US Tiger data and outputs difference as geojson
  files to stdout.

  osmlint missinghighwaysus --bbox="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 usa.mbtiles tiger.mbtiles

osmlint missinglayerbridges

  Identifies bridges with a missing layer tag and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint missinglayerbridges --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint untaggedways

  Identifies ways with no tags at all and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint untaggedways --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint selfintersectinghighways

  Identifies self intersecting highways.

  osmlint selfintersectinghighways --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint nodeendingnearhighway

  Identifies highway nodes ending near another highway, when the two highways don't intersect, and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint nodeendingnearhighway --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint crossingwaterwayshighways

  Identifies faulty intersections of waterways and highways and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint crossingwaterwayshighways --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles

osmlint crossinghighways

  Identifies missing intersections when two highways cross each other and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint crossinghighways --bbox="[62, 31, 70.5, 26.7]" --zoom=12 afghanistan.mbtiles
